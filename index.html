The Problem

Labs are running
blind.

🕵️

No Cross-Lab Visibility

Medicinal, Pharmacognosy, Analysis, Pharmaceutics, Engineering — each lab is a completely isolated silo. Nothing is shared.

📝

Error-Prone Manual Registers

Handwritten records by lab technicians generate 18–25% data inconsistency. Entries are missed, quantities wrong, transfers undocumented.

☠️

Expired Chemicals Go Unnoticed

No automated tracking means chemicals expire on shelves undetected — a safety hazard and a financial drain simultaneously.

🔧

Equipment Breakage Unreported

Damage goes undocumented. Procurement delays stretch 3–8 weeks. Students and researchers stall mid-project.

All lab-registered students and faculty see a new "Lab Management" tab on their existing ERP login. Same credentials, same portal — zero new learning curve. Non-lab users see nothing.

Students search and request. Technicians manage stock. Faculty oversee and approve. Department heads get analytics. All roles are auto-assigned from ERP designation — no manual configuration.
